## Withdrawals FAQ

### Why can't I withdraw after buying or trading an account?
After an account is traded, withdrawals and marketplace sales are locked for **90 days**. This is a security measure and cannot be bypassed or shortened.

You can still play the game normally during this period.

**Also asked as:**

- I transferred my land to a different wallet and can't trade — why?

- How long is the lock period after moving land?
- Why is my account restricted after wallet change?

---

### Why is my withdrawal taking so long? (14-day restriction)
Your IP address may have been previously used by another player. Even if you only play on one device, ISPs assign dynamic IPs that can be shared over time. The system automatically applies a **14-day waiting period** as a security precaution — not a penalty. Once the period ends, your withdrawal will process normally.

**Also asked as:**
- Why do I have to wait 14 days to withdraw?
- Why is my withdrawal delayed due to security?
- Why can't I withdraw right now?

---

### How do I withdraw $FLOWER?
1. Open the **Map**.
2. Go to the **Retreat**.
3. Walk up to the goblin with a wallet icon.
4. Click the **$FLOWER icon**.
5. Choose **Ronin** or **Base Network**.

**Also asked as:**
- How do I cash out FLOWER?
- How do I transfer FLOWER to my wallet?
- How do I move FLOWER off the game?
- How do I withdraw FLOWER to Ronin or Base?

---

### Do I need ETH to withdraw?
Yes. ETH is required to cover gas fees. You must buy ETH on the **Base Network** — ETH from other networks is not accepted.

**Also asked as:**

### How do I fix a "shared network error"?
Wait **14 days** from when the error first appeared. This is the only resolution — no manual fix, IP change, or support intervention can shorten this period.

**Also asked as:**

- How do I resolve shared network error?
- How do I fix shared Wi-Fi withdrawal error?

### Why do I get a "shared network error" even though I'm not sharing my network?
Most ISPs use **dynamic IPs**. If another player used the same IP address recently, the system flags it and applies the 14-day wait automatically.

### How do I deposit $FLOWER into the game?
1. Click the **Gem icon**.
2. Select **Deposit $FLOWER**.

### What tokens can I deposit into the game?
Only **$FLOWER** tokens are accepted. Deposits of any other token (ETH, RON, POL, etc.) are not supported and **cannot be recovered**.

### I accidentally sent ETH or another token — can I get it back?
No. The deposit contract only recognizes $FLOWER. Tokens sent by mistake — including ETH, RON, or POL — are **permanently lost** and cannot be recovered.

### Why is only $FLOWER accepted for deposits?
The deposit contract is built specifically for $FLOWER. It has no mechanism to process or return other tokens.

## Game Errors FAQ

### I can't claim my marketplace sale
Marketplace sales cannot be claimed while you have **pending withdrawals**. Resolve all pending withdrawals first, then try claiming again.

### I get a white screen when opening the app
Clear your browser cache. Cached data conflicting with the game client is the most common cause of a white screen on load.

**What to do:**
1. Clear your browser cache and cookies.
2. Hard-reload the page (Ctrl+Shift+R or Cmd+Shift+R).
3. If using the mobile app, force-close and reopen.

**Also asked as:**
- App stuck on white screen
- Game won't load, only white screen
- Blank screen when opening the game

### My account was banned — was it because of Wi-Fi?
Using shared Wi-Fi does **not** cause a ban. Bans are issued for account sharing, multi-accounting, or violating game rules — not for network type. If your account is locked, submit a support ticket for review.

Permanent bans are rarely overturned.

### Will scanning my face on a new account get me banned again?
No — scanning your face will not cause a new ban. However, verification will not succeed until your existing face verification is cleared from your first account. After that, you must play the new account actively for **30 days** before re-verification is approved.

## Network & Transactions FAQ

### Why is my withdrawal transaction taking so long?
Blockchain transactions can be delayed by network congestion, low gas fees, or wallet/RPC issues.

**What to do:**
1. Check the status in your wallet.
2. Use "Speed Up" to increase the gas price.
3. Switch to a reliable RPC endpoint if available.
4. Wait for congestion to ease, then retry.

**Also asked as:**
- Why is my withdrawal transaction pending?
- Why is my FLOWER transfer stuck?
- Why is my withdrawal not confirmed yet?
- Why is my blockchain transaction delayed?

---

### Why is my transaction failing even though I have enough gas?
Transactions can fail despite sufficient gas due to: network congestion, a gas limit that's too low, nonce conflicts, smart contract errors, or wallet/RPC issues.

**What to do:** Try increasing the gas limit (not just the gas price) and switch to a reliable RPC endpoint.

**Also asked as:**
- Why is my transaction failing with enough gas?
- Why can't I send even though I have gas?
- Why is my transfer stuck despite gas balance?
- Why does my wallet not process transactions?

---

### My transaction is stuck — what do I do?
1. Open your wallet and find the pending transaction.
2. Use **"Cancel"** or **"Speed Up"**.
3. Increase the gas price.
4. Switch to a reliable RPC endpoint.
5. If none work, wait for network congestion to ease and retry.

**Also asked as:**
- How do I unstick a pending transaction?
- What should I do if my transaction is pending forever?
- Pending blockchain transaction not processing
- How do I fix a pending transaction?
- Why won't my transfer confirm?

## Account Rules

### Can I use multiple accounts on the same device?
No. Logging into multiple accounts on the same device results in a **permanent ban**. You may use the same account on different devices you own (phone, tablet, computer), but you cannot run multiple different accounts on one device.

**Also asked as:**

---

### Can someone else do my face verification for me?
No. Face verification must use **your own facial data**. Using another person's face, or having someone verify for you, is considered fraud and results in a **permanent ban** that cannot be reversed.

**Also asked as:**
- Can another person verify my account for me?
- Is it allowed to use someone else's face for verification?

- Will I get banned for using another person's verification?

---

### How many accounts am I allowed?
**One account per player.** Casually switching between multiple accounts is prohibited and results in a ban. If you want to permanently move to a new account, you must first **burn** your current account.

**Also asked as:**
- Can I burn my account to start fresh?
- Is it possible to permanently move to a new account?
- What happens if I casually switch accounts?
- How many accounts are allowed per player?

---

### How do I burn my account?
Send your current account NFT to the designated dead address. **This is permanent and irreversible** — a burned account cannot be retrieved. Only do this if you are certain you want to start fresh on a new account.

**Also asked as:**
- How do I permanently delete my account?

- Can I move to a new account by burning the old one?

### What is funneling and is it bannable?
Funneling is sending tokens from multiple accounts to a single wallet, or using intermediary wallets to consolidate $FLOWER. It is considered an offense. All accounts linked to a funneling wallet may be banned.

**Also asked as:**

### Can I send tokens to a friend's wallet to help them?
Sending tokens to a friend is not automatically bannable, but it is **discouraged**. If it appears suspicious, all linked accounts may be investigated and banned.
