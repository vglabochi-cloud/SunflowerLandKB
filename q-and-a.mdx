## Withdrawals FAQ

### What is the cooldown period after trading an account?  
After an account is traded, the new owner can continue playing the game but cannot sell items or withdraw assets for 90 days. This cooldown period is designed to prevent multi-account exploitation and ensure fair marketplace activity.  

**Irreversible:** The 90-day cooldown is automatically enforced and cannot be bypassed or shortened once an account has been traded.

## Network Transactions FAQ

### Why is my withdrawal transaction taking so long? (network confirmation)
Once your withdrawal is initiated, it must be confirmed on the blockchain. This can take longer if the network is congested, if gas fees are set too low, or if there are wallet/RPC issues. If your transaction is stuck, you can try to speed it up by increasing the gas price or switching to a reliable RPC endpoint.  

**Also asked as:**
- Why is my withdrawal transaction pending?
- Why is my FLOWER transfer stuck on the blockchain?
- Why is my withdrawal not confirmed yet?

---

### I have enough gas fee but cannot proceed
Possible causes:  
- Network congestion  
- Gas limit too low  
- Nonce conflicts  
- Smart contract errors  
- Wallet/RPC issues

---

### What should I do if my transaction is stuck?
1. Check pending transactions.  
2. Use ‚Äúcancel‚Äù or ‚Äúspeed up.‚Äù  
3. Increase gas price.  
4. Switch to a reliable RPC endpoint.  
5. Wait until congestion eases and retry.

### Why does my transaction stay pending?
A transaction remains pending when the gas price is too low or the network is congested. Use the ‚Äúspeed up‚Äù option in your wallet to increase gas price and confirm faster.

**Also asked as:**

### Which networks and gas tokens does the game use?  
The game operates across three networks, each requiring its own gas token. Ronin uses RON for gas, Base uses ETH for gas, and Polygon uses POL for gas. Each network supports specific use cases: Ronin and Base handle $FLOWER deposits and withdrawals, while Polygon is used for store transactions on chain and Sunflower Land NFT transfers.  

**Irreversible:** Transactions cannot be completed without the correct gas token for the chosen network; no alternative method exists.
