### Expansions FAQ

### What is the Basic Island in Sunflower Land?
The Basic Island is the first land expansion available at Prestige 0 in Sunflower Land. It unlocks progressively based on Bumpkin levels, with each stage requiring specific resources and time to complete. Expanding the Basic Island increases farming capacity and resource nodes, making it the foundation for early-game progression.

**Stage-by-stage unlocks:**
- **Bumpkin Level 1:** Unlocks Land 4, requires 3 wood, takes 5 seconds, provides 3 wood nodes.
- **Bumpkin Level 5:** Unlocks Land 5, requires 0.25 coins, takes 5 seconds, adds +8 crop plots (17 total), +1 tree (6 total), +1 stone rock (4 total), +1 iron rock (2 total), +1 gold rock (1 total).
- **Bumpkin Level 6:** Unlocks Land 6, requires 60 coins, takes 1 minute, adds +8 crop plots (25 total), +1 tree (7 total), +1 stone rock (5 total).
- **Bumpkin Level 7:** Unlocks Land 7, requires 5 coins, takes 30 minutes, adds +2 crop plots (27 total), +1 tree (8 total), +1 stone rock (6 total), +1 iron rock (3 total).
- **Bumpkin Level 8:** Unlocks Land 8, requires 3 coins, takes 4 hours, adds +2 crop plots (29 total), +1 tree (9 total), +1 stone rock (7 total), +1 gold rock (2 total).
- **Bumpkin Level 9:** Unlocks Land 9, requires 100 coins, 40 stone, and 5 iron, takes 12 hours, adds +2 crop plots (31 total), finalizing maximum capacity.

**Final Expansion Capacity:**
- 31 crop plots  
- 9 trees  
- 7 stone rocks  
- 4 iron rocks  
- 2 gold rocks  

**Cumulative Requirements:** 60.25 coins, 108 wood, 46 stone, 9 iron, and 1 gold.  
**Total Expansion Time:** Approximately 16 hours and 31 minutes.

**Irreversible:** Once the Basic Island is fully expanded, the maximum number of plots and resource nodes is fixed and cannot be changed.

### How do expansions work in Sunflower Land?
Expansions in Sunflower Land unlock progressively through Bumpkin levels and Prestige milestones. Each expansion adds new land, crop plots, and resource nodes, while later islands introduce advanced features such as flower beds, oil drills, gem mines, and lava pools. Unlocking expansions requires coins, wood, stone, iron, and gold, along with specific time durations. Once an island is fully expanded, its maximum capacity is fixed and cannot be changed.

**Expansion progression path:**
- **Basic Island (Prestige 0):** Unlocks first, provides crop plots, trees, and basic ores.
- **Petal Paradise:** Unlocks after Basic Island, introduces flower beds and expanded farming.
- **Desert Island:** Unlocks after Petal Paradise, adds oil drills and gem mines.
- **Volcano Island:** Unlocks after Desert Island, introduces lava pools and rare volcanic resources.

**Key mechanics:**
- **Bumpkin Levels:** Each expansion stage requires a specific Bumpkin level to unlock.
- **Prestige:** Determines which islands are available; Prestige 0 starts with Basic Island.
- **Resources & Time:** Each stage requires coins and materials, plus a set expansion time.
- **Finality:** Once expanded, the maximum plots and nodes are permanent.

**Irreversible:** Expansion limits are permanent; once an island is fully expanded, its maximum plots and nodes cannot be altered.

### What is the Prestige system in Sunflower Land?
Prestige in Sunflower Land is a progression reset mechanic that allows players to restart their farming journey after reaching certain milestones. By entering Prestige mode, a player resets their land expansions, crops, and resources, but gains permanent Prestige benefits that unlock new islands, features, and advanced gameplay. Prestige is designed to extend long-term progression and keep farming challenging and rewarding.

**Key details:**
- **Unlock Requirement:** Prestige becomes available after reaching specific farming levels and completing prior islands.
- **Reset Effect:** All land expansions, crops, and resources are reset to the starting state.
- **Permanent Benefits:** Prestige unlocks new islands (e.g., Petal Paradise, Desert Island, Volcano Island) and grants access to advanced features such as flower farming, oil drills, gem mines, and lava pools.
- **Progression Path:** Each Prestige tier expands gameplay depth, requiring higher Bumpkin levels and more resources to progress.
- **Strategic Choice:** Players must decide when to Prestige, balancing the loss of current progress against the benefits of unlocking new content.

**Irreversible:** Once Prestige is activated, all prior progress is reset and cannot be recovered.

### What are Bumpkin levels in Sunflower Land?
Bumpkin levels represent a player‚Äôs farming experience and progression in Sunflower Land. As players perform farming activities such as planting, harvesting, and crafting, they gain experience points (XP) that increase their Bumpkin level. Higher levels unlock new land expansions, crops, and features, making Bumpkin progression essential for advancing through the game.

**Key details:**
- **Experience Growth:** Bumpkin levels increase through farming actions, crafting, and resource collection.
- **Expansion Unlocks:** Each land expansion stage requires a minimum Bumpkin level (e.g., Basic Island starts at Level 1, Petal Paradise begins at Level 10, Desert Island at Level 16, Volcano Island at Level 22).
- **Gameplay Benefits:** Higher levels unlock new crops, resource nodes, and advanced features such as flower beds, oil drills, gem mines, and lava pools.
- **Progression Path:** Bumpkin levels act as milestones that guide players through the expansion system and overall game progression.
- **Permanent Growth:** Once a Bumpkin level is achieved, it remains tied to the player‚Äôs account and cannot be reduced.

**Irreversible:** Bumpkin levels only increase through gameplay and cannot be reset or lowered.

### What resources are used for expansions in Sunflower Land?
Expansions in Sunflower Land require a combination of coins and materials such as wood, stone, iron, and gold. These resources are consumed at each stage of land expansion, with requirements increasing as players progress to higher Bumpkin levels and more advanced islands. Managing resources efficiently is essential for unlocking new plots, nodes, and features.

**Key resources:**
- **Coins:** The primary currency used for most expansion stages, increasing significantly with higher islands.
- **Wood:** Required for early expansions, especially on the Basic Island, and gathered from trees.
- **Stone:** Introduced in mid-game expansions, used heavily in Petal Paradise and Desert Island.
- **Iron:** Needed for advanced expansions, particularly Desert and Volcano Islands, and mined from iron rocks.
- **Gold:** A rare resource required for later stages of Petal Paradise, Desert Island, and Volcano Island expansions.

**Resource progression:**
- **Basic Island:** Small amounts of coins, wood, stone, iron, and gold.
- **Petal Paradise:** Larger coin costs, with wood and stone requirements increasing.
- **Desert Island:** Heavy stone and iron requirements, plus multiple gold costs.
- **Volcano Island:** Very high coin, stone, iron, and gold requirements, representing peak expansion difficulty.

**Irreversible:** Resources spent on expansions cannot be refunded or recovered once used.
